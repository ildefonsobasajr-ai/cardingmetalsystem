Who We Are

ABOUT US

CARDING METAL SYSTEM AND TRADING CORPORATION was established in 1967 by our visionary leader Ricardo "Carding" Ng. We offer a comprehensive range of fabrication and machining services under one roof — from general machine shop works, stamping and punch press, tool and die making, shearing and bending, and structural fabrication, to precision gear and sprocket fabrication, CNC machining (lathe and milling), and professional welding services. Whether your project calls for high-volume production or custom one-off components, we have the expertise and equipment to deliver.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing our clients with high-quality manufacturing services and are dedicated to continuously improving our methods to produce excellent metal products. Our goal is to achieve a level of distinction by aligning our standards with those of leading global firms. Today, we are engaged in numerous projects, both major and minor, and remain steadfast in delivering craftsmanship that meets global demand.

Our Vision

From the precision of a single machined component to the scale of a full structural assembly, we bring the same dedication, skill, and integrity that have defined our shop since 1967. We are not just a fabrication facility — we are a partner in your success, committed to delivering quality work that stands the test of time.
